Pricing Packages (Production Stages)

These are not “services”. Each plan delivers your company at a specific readiness level. Higher plans include everything from the earlier stages.

Stage 1 — Solo Company

Legal + professional basics. Ready to invoice and operate.

Stage 2 — Operating Company

Website/store + compliance + social presence. Ready to sell.

Stage 3 — Growth-Ready Company

Systems + integrations + launch. Ready to scale.

Solo Company

Stage 1

For: side hustles, consultants, cleaners, freelancers, one-person businesses.

You get
  • Company registration Enskild Firma or Aktiebolag + F-skatt + VAT registration (if needed).
  • Basic brand kit Simple logo + color palette + business card design.
  • One-page website Who you are + what you offer + contact form (conversion-focused).
  • Accounting system setup Software setup + chart of accounts + invoice templates ready.

Result

You can legally operate, invoice clients, and look professional.

Operating Company

Stage 2

For: e-commerce, agencies, small teams, local businesses that must sell immediately.

You get
  • Everything in Solo Company Includes all Stage 1 deliverables.
  • Full website or basic store 5-page website (Home, Services, About, Contact, Legal) OR basic e-commerce setup.
  • Legal documents Privacy policy (GDPR) + Terms & Conditions.
  • Social media presence pack Instagram + Facebook pages setup + profile design + 15 branded ready-to-post posts.
  • Accounting included (initial period) Bookkeeping handled for a short kickoff phase, then moves to monthly subscription.

Result

Your business looks real, active, compliant, and ready to sell.

Growth-Ready Company

Stage 3

For: startups, high-volume retail, founders building systems & scaling fast.

You get
  • Everything in Operating Company Includes Stage 1 + Stage 2 deliverables.
  • Custom digital solution Advanced e-commerce OR custom web application (defined scope).
  • Integrations Payments + shipping + key business tools (as needed for the scope).
  • Marketing launch (time-boxed) Ad accounts setup + campaign structure + first month management.
  • Priority delivery Dedicated project lead + faster response and execution cadence.
  • Extended accounting (initial period) Accounting included for a longer kickoff phase, then monthly subscription.

Result

A company built to scale—systems in place, ready for growth.

Important: Packages are fixed. If it’s not listed, it’s not included. Custom work is handled only through a separate agreement.
