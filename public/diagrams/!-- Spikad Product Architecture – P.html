Product Architecture (Production Stages)

This defines exactly what each stage guarantees — and what it does not. If it’s not written here, it’s not included.

Stage 1 — Solo Company

Company exists. Ready to invoice and operate.

Stage 2 — Operating Company

Company operates. Ready to sell and look active.

Stage 3 — Growth-Ready Company

Company scales. Systems and launch in place.

Stage 1 — Solo Company

Company Birth

Purpose: Create a legally existing, professional company that can invoice and operate.

✅ Guaranteed Outcome: A legally registered company + professional presence + accounting system ready to use.

Guaranteed Deliverables
  • Legal & Registration Company registered (EF or AB) + F-skatt completed + VAT registered if applicable.
  • Brand Basics 1 logo + 1 color palette + 1 business card design.
  • Web Presence One-page website: Who we are + What we do + Contact form.
  • Accounting Setup Software account created + chart of accounts + invoice templates prepared.
Not Included
  • No ongoing accounting Bookkeeping is not included in Stage 1.
  • No marketing or ads No campaigns, no strategy, no performance promises.
  • No social media setup No pages and no content in Stage 1.
  • No custom work / unlimited revisions Only what is listed above. Fixed scope.

Exit Criteria: Registration confirmed + website live + accounting access delivered.

Stage 2 — Operating Company

Company Operates

Purpose: Turn a registered company into an operating business that can sell and look active.

✅ Guaranteed Outcome: Sales surface + legal compliance + social presence + accounting running (initial period).

Guaranteed Deliverables
  • Includes Stage 1 All deliverables from Solo Company are included.
  • Website or Basic Store 5-page website (Home, Services, About, Contact, Legal) OR basic e-commerce setup.
  • Legal Compliance Privacy Policy (GDPR) + Terms & Conditions published.
  • Social Presence Pack Instagram + Facebook pages + profile setup + 15 branded ready-to-post posts.
  • Accounting Included (Initial) Bookkeeping handled for a defined kickoff period, then transitions to subscription.
Not Included
  • No ongoing social posting We deliver pages + posts. We do not manage them long-term.
  • No growth strategy / guarantees No promises about sales, followers, or results.
  • No custom copywriting Client provides basic business info. We structure it.
  • No unlimited revisions Fixed scope. Changes require a change order.

Exit Criteria: Website/store live + legal docs published + social pages live + 15 posts delivered + accounting kickoff completed.

Stage 3 — Growth-Ready Company

Company Scales

Purpose: Systemize the business so it can scale without breaking.

✅ Guaranteed Outcome: Scalable tech + integrations + time-boxed launch + extended accounting kickoff.

Guaranteed Deliverables
  • Includes Stage 2 All deliverables from Operating Company are included.
  • Custom Digital Solution Advanced e-commerce OR custom web application (scope locked before start).
  • Integrations Payments + shipping + approved business tools integrated.
  • Marketing Launch (Time-Boxed) Ad accounts setup + campaign structure + first month management only.
  • Priority Execution Dedicated project owner + priority delivery lane.
  • Accounting Included (Extended) Accounting handled for a longer kickoff period, then transitions to subscription.
Not Included
  • No open-ended development No endless features. Scope is fixed before work begins.
  • No ongoing marketing management Launch is time-boxed. Long-term marketing is separate.
  • No fundraising / hiring We don’t do investor outreach or recruit teams inside this stage.
  • No pivots mid-delivery Changing direction requires a new scope and a new agreement.

Exit Criteria: Tech delivered as scoped + integrations live + launch month completed + accounting transition in place.

Global Rules (Apply to All Stages)

  • Higher stages include lower stages. No stage can be skipped.
  • No custom work inside stages. If it’s not listed, it’s not included.
  • Exceptions are logged, not executed. The system is improved between batches.
  • Scope changes require a change order. Even if the invoice is 0 SEK.
One line definition: Spikad delivers companies at fixed readiness levels through a controlled production system.
